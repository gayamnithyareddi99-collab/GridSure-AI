Capability 01 · Coordinate Intelligence

AI Setting-Out Assurance Engine

Automatically analyses raw coordinate tables and CAD files to cross-correlate and reveal geometric conflicts, mathematical outliers, and drafting discrepancies before layout markers are staked on site.

The AI Setting-Out Assurance Engine ingests multi-format design files (DWG, DXF, CSV coordinate schedules, BIM models) and constructs a dynamic project layout fingerprint for each construction site learning coordinate-transformation baselines, instrument-shifting tolerances, and sub-surface geotechnical anomalies. The system automatically flags inconsistencies, generating warning notifications and remedial suggestions that prevent structural misalignment before physical execution begins.
Capability 02 · Drift Detection

Tolerance Drift Monitoring System

Aggregates spatial coordinate patterns from on-site layout clusters to detect small systematic directional biases such as uncalibrated robotic total stations that pass individual checks but degrade structural grid lines.

Traditional quality checks verify individual points in isolation. GridSure AI's Tolerance Drift Monitoring System aggregates all spatial coordinate patterns to identify cumulative directional bias (e.g., a 3mm drift repeated across 12 piles adds up to structural misalignment of 36mm). The system detects systematic equipment errors such as uncalibrated Leica or Trimble total stations and alerts field teams to recalibrate before excavation or concrete pour, preventing catastrophic structural failure.
Capability 03 · Vision AI

Site-Mark Intelligence System

Advanced mobile computer vision automatically processes, digitises, and classifies hand-marked survey stakes from standard smartphone photos enabling direct comparison of physical realities with digital design registries.

The Site-Mark Intelligence System eliminates the need for specialist survey instruments to verify physical peg markings. Site engineers simply photograph survey stakes, pins, and level markings on-site and upload them via the mobile app. The vision AI automatically reads cut/fill notations, coordinate offsets, and grid IDs from the image, cross-references them against the active CAD register, and immediately flags any mismatches turning a smartphone into a precision spatial audit tool.
Capability 04 · Drawing Sync

Revision-Sensitive Setting-Out Verification

Automatically syncs with live Common Data Environments (CDEs) to instantly alert field engineers if they are setting out from outdated or superseded drawing revisions.

34% of UK setting-out failures occur because field teams execute layouts using obsolete paper plans. GridSure AI's Revision-Sensitive Verification module integrates directly with Procore, Autodesk Construction Cloud, and Dropbox automatically checking drawing file IDs and revision numbers against the current master drawing register. If a site engineer uploads a coordinate schedule from an outdated revision, the platform instantly alerts the field team before any physical markers are staked.
Capability 05 · ESG Analytics

Material Rework Carbon & Cost Estimator

Converts layout-accuracy failure risks into the volume of concrete, steel reinforcement, and earthworks fuel that would be wasted on rework translating geometric deviations into tonnes of CO₂-equivalent avoided.

GridSure AI is not just a cost management tool it is a sustainability platform. The Material Rework Carbon Estimator translates every millimetre deviation into precise material waste calculations (volume of concrete, steel re-fabrication, machinery fuel) using proprietary algorithms mapped to UK supply chain emission factors. This enables contractors to generate audit-ready ESG reports demonstrating carbon offset savings through early-stage error prevention, directly supporting Building Safety Act 2022 Golden Thread compliance.
Capability 06 · Compliance Reporting

"Golden Thread" Compliance Ledger

Organises and indexes timestamped, certified setting-out reports creating an immutable digital ledger fully compliant with the UK Building Safety Act 2022 and structural audit guidelines.

The Golden Thread Compliance Ledger automatically converts raw on-site coordinate checks and mobile peg photos into timestamped, branded layout compliance certificates meeting the strict requirements of the UK Building Safety Regulator (BSR), British Standards BS 5964 and BS EN 1090, and NHBC foundation guidelines. Every spatial deviation, drawing revision flag, and tolerance alert is permanently stored with a structured audit trail minimising contractor liability and insurance friction during structural handovers.

How does the Tolerance Drift Monitoring System work in practice?

+
The Tolerance Drift Monitoring System aggregates all spatial coordinate inputs from active layout points on a live site to detect systematic directional biases that individual quality checks miss. For example: a robotic total station that is 1mm out of horizontal calibration will produce a 3mm error at 3 metres, a 15mm error at 15 metres, and a 30mm error across a full structural grid each individual reading passing inspection but the cumulative pattern revealing a systematic instrument error. The platform detects this pattern early and alerts the field team to recalibrate the instrument before excavation or foundation pour begins, preventing structural misalignment that would otherwise require expensive demolition.

What happens when a coordinate error or drawing revision mismatch is detected?

+
The system immediately generates a structured, plain-language alert specifying exactly which coordinate, gridline, or drawing revision is flagged, the magnitude of the deviation, the applicable British Standard tolerance threshold, and a recommended remedial action all within 2–3 minutes of data upload. Field teams and project managers receive real-time notifications via the cloud dashboard and mobile app. The alert is permanently logged with a timestamp and the original uploaded data in the Golden Thread compliance ledger, creating an unalterable audit record for structural insurers, Building Safety Regulators, and third-party handover inspectors.

How does GridSure AI help with UK Building Safety Act 2022 compliance?

+
The UK Building Safety Act 2022 legally mandates a digital, audit-ready "Golden Thread" of structural compliance records for all high-risk residential buildings and wider commercial infrastructure developments. GridSure AI automatically converts every coordinate audit, peg verification, tolerance drift alert, and drawing revision check into a timestamped, certified layout compliance certificate aligned with the Building Safety Regulator's (BSR) verification frameworks, British Standards BS 5964 and BS EN 1090, and NHBC Technical Standards. Subcontractors can directly use these certificates to demonstrate active compliance during site inspections, structural handovers, and insurance assessments dramatically reducing liability exposure and administrative burden.

How does GridSure AI help reduce insurance premiums?

+
Structural warranty providers, national insurance bodies such as NHBC, and commercial insurers increasingly require demonstrable evidence of active pre-pour quality governance to justify premium reductions. GridSure AI generates a continuous, structured audit trail of pre-pour coordinate verifications, tolerance drift monitoring, drawing revision compliance checks, and physical peg confirmations all with timestamps and spatial accuracy scores. By presenting this data as a legally defensible risk mitigation history, subcontractors can negotiate lower structural liability and project insurance premiums delivering a measurable financial return that typically covers the entire SaaS subscription cost many times over on a single project.
